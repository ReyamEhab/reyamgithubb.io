About Arch

Two years ago, I was a student passionate about architecture, and I studied architecture for two years.

Kindergarten

The concept here was inspired by leaves. I chose this idea to make the child integrate with nature.
I showed this idea in the main entrance to the kindergarten. Here we will see that there are two
levels for the entrance so one for the teachers and one for the student. The landscape was also
inspired by the shape of the leaves and there are passages for the veins of the leaves. Some of
the discharges that you see in the ceilings and the green color of the glass make it more imme
rsed in the beauty of nature.
